Setup and Install printer

Setting up your printer with two steps: First Connect your printer to computer then Install Printer Driver.

For Computer (Windows)

  1. Unboxing: Unpack your Epson printer and remove all protective materials.
  2. Connections: Plug in the power cord and turn on the printer. Connect it to your computer using a USB cable.
  3. Driver Installation: Visit the Epson website to download the latest drivers for your printer model. Follow the on-screen instructions to install the drivers.
  4. Printer Setup: Go to Control Panel > Devices and Printers. Click "Add a printer" and follow the prompts. Select your Epson printer from the list.
  5. Test Print: Print a test page to ensure the setup is successful.

For Mac

  1. Unboxing: Remove packaging materials from the Epson printer.
  2. Connections: Connect the printer to a power source and turn it on. Use a USB cable to connect it to your Mac.
  3. Driver Installation: Go to the Epson website and download the appropriate drivers for your printer model. Install the drivers by following the on-screen instructions.
  4. Adding Printer: Open System Preferences on your Mac and select "Printers & Scanners." Click the "+" button to add a new printer. Your Epson printer should appear in the list. Select it and click "Add."
  5. Test Print: Print a test page to ensure the setup is successful.

Steps to Setup Wireless Epson Printer

Follow easy steps to easily setup the Epson printer to Wi-Fi network:

  • Turn on the wireless printer.
  • Connect to Wi-Fi network using printer's control panel.
  • Install printer drivers on computer.
  • Select wireless printer during installation.
  • Complete setup; printer ready.

Steps to Setup Wired Epson Printer

Follow easy steps to easily setup the Epson printer to wired connection:

  • Connect the printer to the router.
  • Turn on the printer and press the LAN button.
  • Select the ‘LAN Setup’.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup process.
